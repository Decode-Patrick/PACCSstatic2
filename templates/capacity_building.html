Capacity Building

Sustainable Development Goal 13 (SDG Goal 13) on climate action calls for global outcomes. However, most implementation will be local. And experiences of climate change impacts are place specific. Partnerships (SDG Goal 17) and a ‘local lens’ focusing on indicators for local action, adaptation and capacity is therefore crucial for informing global approaches to addressing climate change.

Capacity building activities in Somaliland are a key pillar of PACCS. They centre on strengthening research, data management and training capacities of SORADI and UoH, research–based education, and research collaboration (local, regional and North-South).

PACCS capacity building activities have synergies with and contribute to the Danida-funded program Building Stronger Universities IV Building Stronger Universities, IV — Roskilde University Research Portal (ruc.dk), and currently focuses on creating a Master Program on climate change and environmental science.
